ADMISSION PROCEDURE

A). Upon the payment of prescribed admission processing fee, the applicant shall be given prospectus containing Admission Form. The applicant shall fill the form in his own handwriting and shall submit the same in admission section by due date along with the following attested pre-requisite. Four recent passport size photographs Certificates/degrees of academics along with DMCs Character certificate from the institution last attended Migration certificate for students who are not registered with the University (after admission) An undertaking on stamp paper duly signed and stamped by the Class Magistrate (after admission) Domicile certificate CNIC of applicant/Father/Guardian Undertaking on stamp paper duly signed by oath commissioner.

B). Incomplete forms shall not be entertained


ADMISSION TEST

The candidate seeking admission in the UST Bannu will have to pass Entry Test for admission in all disciplines. The candidate shall fill in the test Registration Forms and shall send it along with a bank draft of prescribed fee and passport size photograph to Pakistan Testing Service, Islamabad. Entry shall be conduct by Pakistan Testing Service, Pakistan at the Main Campus of the UST Bannu. The duration of the test will be two hours. Applicant will be required to reach the test centre on the date and time specified on the Entry Test Card. Late corners will not be given permission. Entry to the test centre will only be allowed on the production of the Entry Test Roll No. Slip. Use of the calculators, clipboard, foot and other such materials are not allowed in the test.


RE-ADMISSION

The candidate aggrieved by the decision of the admission committee on any of the foregoing clauses may go on appeal to the Vice Chancellor whose decision shall be final, and he may in his discretion: Re-admit the applicant Reject the appeal Re-admit the applicant subject to the payment of fine, which may extend to Rs.15,000/-
